# Molecular gold strings: aurophilicity, luminescence and structure–property correlations

## Cite_Key
seifert2020molecular

## Problem
Understanding how spatial geometry (gold-gold distances) and electronic structure of one-dimensional gold strings determine their optical and electronic properties.

## Method
Review of one-dimensional molecular gold strings analyzing emission energy dependence on gold-gold distance and electronic structure, including perturbation by external stimuli (solvent, pH, pressure).

## Data
Gold-gold distances in molecular strings; emission energies; structure-property correlation data under various perturbations.

## Metrics
Emission wavelength; Au-Au distances; luminescence quantum yield; structure-property correlation coefficients.

## Findings
Emission energy in molecular gold strings depends directly on gold-gold distance and the electronic structure of the whole string. External stimuli (solvent, pH, pressure) tune properties via geometry changes, demonstrating direct structure-property correlations.

## Limitations
Review study; limited quantitative predictive models; gold-specific interactions may not generalize.

## Citation
