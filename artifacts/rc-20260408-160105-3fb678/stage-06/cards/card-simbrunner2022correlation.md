# Correlation between two- and three-dimensional crystallographic lattices for epitaxial analysis. II. Experimental results

## Cite_Key
simbrunner2022correlation

## Problem
Determining how 2D monolayer lattice geometry correlates with 3D crystal lattice geometry in epitaxial growth systems.

## Method
Experimental epitaxial growth studies comparing 2D surface lattice geometry parameters with 3D bulk crystal lattice geometry using crystallographic analysis.

## Data
2D monolayer lattice parameters; 3D crystal lattice parameters; epitaxial relationship data; geometric correlations.

## Metrics
Lattice mismatch parameters; epitaxial relationship classifications; geometric correlation measures between 2D and 3D lattices.

## Findings
Direct correlations exist between 2D monolayer lattice geometry and 3D crystal lattice geometry in epitaxial growth. Surface geometry parameters can predict and map to bulk crystal properties through established geometric relationships.

## Limitations
Experimental study limited to specific epitaxial systems; generalizability across different material classes requires further validation.

## Citation
Simbrunner, J., et al. (2022). Acta Crystallographica Section A. doi:10.1107/s2053273322002170
