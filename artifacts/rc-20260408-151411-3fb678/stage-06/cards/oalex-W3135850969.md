# In the realm of the Hubble tension—a review of solutions

## Cite_Key
valentino2021realm

## Problem
Significant tension (4σ to 6σ) exists between early-universe predictions and late-universe measurements of the Hubble constant (H0).

## Method
Thorough review of the Hubble tension problem and classification of more than 1000 proposed theoretical solutions.

## Data
Cosmological observations (Planck 2018, BAO, Pantheon, SH0ES).

## Metrics
Hubble constant (H0) value, tension level (σ).

## Findings
Reviews solutions including early/late dark energy, modified gravity, and neutrino interactions. Some models (e.g., early dark energy) reduce tension to 1-2σ, but no single proposal is definitively superior.
