# Covalent organic frameworks: an ideal platform for designing ordered materials and advanced applications

## Cite_Key
liu2020covalent

## Problem
Designing organic materials with predictable structures and unique properties/functions.

## Method
Comprehensive review of the structural design, synthetic control, and functional exploration of Covalent Organic Frameworks (COFs).

## Findings
COFs enable the predesigning of both primary and high-order structures. Well-defined skeletons, pores, and interfaces in COFs provide a chemical basis to control interactions with photons, excitons, and molecules, leading to unique functions.
