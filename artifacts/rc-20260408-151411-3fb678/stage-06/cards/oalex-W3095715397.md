# Emerging Chiral Materials

## Cite_Key
kuang2020emerging

## Problem
Fabricating chiral nanostructures with high enantiomeric purity and understanding the transfer of chirality from nanoscale to macroscale remains challenging.

## Method
Review of the state-of-the-art in chiral materials, covering synthesis, assemblies, reconfigurable chirality, origin of symmetry breaking, theory calculations, and applications.

## Data
Review of current literature on chiral nanomaterials.

## Metrics
Enantiomeric purity, chiroptical properties, dissymmetry factor.

## Findings
Highlights advances in chiral supramolecular materials, nanocrystals, and assemblies. Theoretical and experimental techniques are uncovering the origins of chirality. Future opportunities include quantum computing, biomedical imaging, and optoelectronics.

## Limitations
The ability to fabricate or assemble complex chiral structures with high control in enantiomeric purity is an intrinsic challenge.
