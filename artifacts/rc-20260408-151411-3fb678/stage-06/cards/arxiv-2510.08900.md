# Few-shot Molecular Property Prediction: A Survey

## Cite_Key
wang2025fewshot

## Problem
Molecular property prediction is limited by scarce annotations and labeled data due to high-cost wet-lab experiments.

## Method
First comprehensive survey of Few-shot Molecular Property Prediction (FSMPP), introducing a unified taxonomy of methods.

## Data
Review of existing FSMPP studies and benchmarks.

## Metrics
N/A

## Findings
Core challenges include cross-property generalization under distribution shifts and cross-molecule generalization under structural heterogeneity. Methods are organized into data, model, and learning paradigm levels.
