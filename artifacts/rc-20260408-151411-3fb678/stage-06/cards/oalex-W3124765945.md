# Spatiotemporal molecular medicine: A new era of clinical and translational medicine

## Cite_Key
wang2021spatiotemporal

## Problem
Traditional medicine often lacks the integration of dynamic and spatial molecular data with clinical phenotypes for precise understanding of disease pathogenesis.

## Method
Proposes the conceptual framework of 'spatiotemporal molecular medicine' integrating clinical spatialization, temporalization, phenome, and molecular multi-omics.

## Data
Spatial transcriptomics/proteomics, clinical phenome data.

## Metrics
N/A

## Findings
Spatiotemporal molecular medicine provides a four-dimensional and dynamical picture of disease. It allows for the precise identification of cellular dysfunction, cell-cell communication, and tumor microenvironment dynamics.

## Limitations
Requires rapid development of artificial intelligence, automatic robots, and standardized protocols for measurements and analyses to overcome clinical application barriers.
