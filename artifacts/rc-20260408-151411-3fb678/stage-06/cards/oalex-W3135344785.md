# Light-matter coupling and quantum geometry in moiré materials

## Cite_Key
topp2021lightmatter

## Problem
Understanding how light couples to flat bands in moiré materials, where electronic kinetic energy is quenched.

## Method
Theoretical analysis of intraband quantum metric and interband Berry connection in model systems (sawtooth quantum chain, twisted bilayer graphene).

## Data
Tight-binding model simulations.

## Metrics
Light-matter coupling matrix elements, Floquet topological gap.

## Findings
Flat bands have nonvanishing light-matter coupling via geometric contributions: intraband quantum metric allows diamagnetic coupling, and interband Berry connection governs dipole matrix elements. Floquet-topological gap can open in twisted bilayer graphene despite nearly vanishing Fermi velocity.
