# Facile Access to Polar-Functionalized Ultrahigh Molecular Weight Polyethylene at Ambient Conditions

## Cite_Key
hu2021facile

## Problem
Synthesis of high-molecular-weight polar copolymers via coordination–insertion polymerization is difficult, usually requiring high pressures or resulting in low molecular weights.

## Method
Design of a family of well-designed α-diimine nickel catalysts with a concerted double-layer steric strategy for coordination–insertion copolymerization.

## Data
Polymerization reaction data at ambient conditions.

## Metrics
Number-average molecular weight (Mn), catalytic activity, number of polar units incorporated.

## Findings
Generated polar-functionalized ultrahigh molecular weight polyethylenes (UHMWPEs, Mn up to 1.10 × 10^6 g mol−1) at ambient conditions (1 bar, 30 °C). The catalyst Ni4 produced the highest Mn (6.04 × 10^6 g mol−1).
