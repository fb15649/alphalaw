# Extended tight‐binding quantum chemistry methods

## Cite_Key
bannwarth2020extended

## Problem
Need for computationally efficient and reasonably accurate quantum chemistry methods for large molecules.

## Method
Review of the extended tight-binding (xTB) family of semiempirical methods, including parameterization and benchmarking.

## Data
Gas phase theoretical reference data for geometries, frequencies, and noncovalent interactions.

## Metrics
Geometries, vibrational frequencies, noncovalent interactions accuracy.

## Findings
xTB methods offer parameter availability for almost the entire periodic table and a consistent parameterization strategy. They provide an excellent cost-accuracy ratio and high robustness for various chemical research applications.
