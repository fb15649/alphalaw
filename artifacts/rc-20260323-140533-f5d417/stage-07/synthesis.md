## Cluster 1: Einstein-Aether Modified Gravity Framework

**Core Thesis**: Aether exists as a dynamical vector field within general relativity, breaking local Lorentz symmetry while preserving mathematical consistency.

**Key Sources**: Gurses et al. (2024), Efremova & Balakin (2023), Balakin & Shakirzyanov (2023), Zhang et al. (2023), Mukherjee et al. (2024), Hajian (2025), Chan et al. (2022)

**Synthesized Findings**:
- **Minimal Einstein-Aether theory** reduces to Einstein equations with perfect fluid contribution, providing natural mechanism for cosmological constant through local Lorentz symmetry breaking (Gurses 2024)
- **Aether flow invariants** (expansion, shear, vorticity) characterize the medium; axion coupling to shear removes degeneracies in the theory (Efremova 2023, Balakin 2023)
- **Black hole solutions** exist with modified horizon structures including "universal horizons" and potential naked singularities; Birkhoff's theorem does not hold in standard form (Zhang 2023, Chan 2022)
- **Gauge structure** revealed: aether vector field is Hodge dual to gauge field, establishing new symmetry relating to cosmological constant mechanism (Hajian 2025)
- **Cosmological constraints** from modified Chaplygin gas models and black hole accretion provide observational bounds on aether parameters (Mukherjee 2024)

## Cluster 3: Michelson-Morley Reinterpretation

**Core Thesis**: The MMX "null result" was misinterpreted; aether-compatible explanations exist through statistical reanalysis, higher-order effects, and Earth-attached aether models.

**Key Sources**: Mikel/Aickin (2025), Stávek (2023), Singh (2024), Soltani (2025), Ilie (2025)

**Synthesized Findings**:
- **Statistical reanalysis** suggests MMX may have discovered the effect sought; "null result" interpretation potentially based on flawed statistical methodology (Aickin 2025)
- **Fourth-order effects** (v/c)⁴ predicted in MMX under stationary aether assumption; LIGO sensitivity sufficient to detect predicted fringe shifts (Stávek 2023)
- **Special relativity critique**: SR itself fails to provide satisfactory explanation for MMX null result when examined critically; both classical and SR frameworks have explanatory gaps (Singh 2024)
- **Earth-attached aether** (AATE) theory: If luminiferous aether attaches to Earth's center, relative velocity is zero, naturally explaining null result (Soltani 2025)
- **Superfluid vacuum compatibility**: MMX null result compatible with quantum vacuum framework because superfluid medium does not produce expected drag effects (Ilie 2025)

**Critical Insight**: Multiple independent frameworks converge on the conclusion that MMX does not falsify aether—whether through statistical flaws, higher-order undetected effects, or medium properties preventing detection.

**Key Sources**: Annila & Wikström (2022), Sbitnev (2021), Khugaev & Bibaeva (2023), Agrawal (2021)

**Synthesized Findings**:
- **Vacuum quanta framework**: Dark matter and dark energy represent gravitational effects of expanding universe embodied in vacuum quanta as ground-state field (Annila 2022)
- **Superfluid ether manifestation**: Dark matter may be observable effect of superfluid ether dynamics; gravity has torsion component analogous to electromagnetism (Sbitnev 2021)
- **Dual ether forms**: Ether exists in two forms as fundamental physical reality; DM/DE explained through ether properties without requiring new particles (Khugaev 2023)
- **Space fabric model**: Cosmological substances in space fabric medium explain gravitation and cosmic phenomena (Agrawal 2021)

**Unification Potential**: All sources converge on aether/vacuum as explanatory substrate for cosmological anomalies currently attributed to dark matter/energy.

## Gap 1: Quantitative Aether Density Derivation

**Description**: No framework provides first-principles quantitative derivation of aether density. Einstein-Aether theory treats density as effective parameter; superfluid theories imply density without calculating it.

**Evidence**: 
- Gurses (2024): "perfect fluid contribution" without density value
- Sbitnev (2021): Superfluid properties assumed, not derived
- Johansson (2020): Compression energy requires density as input

**Impact**: Critical for connecting aether theory to measurable cosmological parameters and vacuum energy density calculations (~10⁻⁹ J/m³ observed vs. ~10¹¹³ J/m³ QFT prediction).

## Gap 2: Unified Viscosity Characterization

**Description**: Aether viscosity treated inconsistently across frameworks—zero for superfluid models, non-zero for Einstein-Aether shear terms, unaddressed elsewhere.

**Evidence**:
- Superfluid theory: Zero viscosity fundamental (Sbitnev 2021, Ilie 2025)
- Einstein-Aether: Shear viscosity implicit in flow invariants (Efremova 2023)
- No framework derives viscosity from first principles or provides testable predictions

**Impact**: Viscosity determines energy dissipation, wave propagation characteristics, and could distinguish between aether models experimentally.

## Gap 3: Permittivity-Permeability-Medium Unification

## Gap 4: QFT Vacuum Energy Integration

## Gap 5: Experimental Discrimination Protocols

**Description**: No proposed experiments can quantitatively measure aether properties (density, elasticity, viscosity) or definitively distinguish aether theories from standard physics.

**Evidence**:
- Stávek (2023): LIGO test proposed but for fringe shift, not property measurement
- All frameworks: Predictions either match SR/GR exactly or remain untested
- No dedicated aether detection experiment protocols

**Impact**: Without testable quantitative predictions that differ from standard physics, aether theories remain interpretational alternatives rather than falsifiable physical theories.

## Gap 7: Cosmological Constant Resolution Quantification

**Evidence**:

## Synthesis Conclusion

The pro-aether literature demonstrates significant theoretical sophistication across multiple frameworks, with Einstein-Aether theory providing the most mathematically rigorous foundation and superfluid vacuum theory offering the most direct derivation of electromagnetic properties. However, critical gaps remain:

1. **No unified framework** derives ALL physical properties (density, elasticity, viscosity, permittivity) from single set of principles
2. **Quantitative predictions** that could falsify standard physics are underdeveloped
3. **QFT integration** remains the largest unexplored opportunity for decisive evidence

The strongest convergence occurs around:
- **MMX reinterpretation** (multiple independent challenges to null result)
- **EM constants from medium** (elastic properties derivation)
- **Dark matter/energy as aether effects** (cosmological unification)

Priority should be given to **Gap 4 (QFT integration)** and **Gap 5 (experimental protocols)** as these offer the highest potential for transforming aether theory from interpretational alternative to falsifiable physical framework.
