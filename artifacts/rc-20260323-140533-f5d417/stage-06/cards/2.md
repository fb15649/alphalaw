# The quantum luminiferous aether

## Cite_Key
larson2024quantum

## Problem
Need for unified physical framework explaining electromagnetism, gravity, and quantum phenomena without relativity's conceptual problems

## Method
Postulates solid two-component quantum luminiferous aether; derives fundamental equations from aether properties using mathematical formalism

## Data
Standard electromagnetic and gravitational phenomena

## Metrics
Derivation of Maxwell's equations; Lorentz force; Newton's gravitation; consistency with observed phenomena

## Findings
Maxwell's equations, Lorentz force, and Newton's gravitation derivable from aether postulates; dark matter may be explained through aether framework; absolute realist model possible

## Limitations
Replaces well-established relativity theory; limited citation count (2); published in alternative physics venue
