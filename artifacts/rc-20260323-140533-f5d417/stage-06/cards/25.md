# The Descartes Code (Spin Orbital Rotation of Photons)–I. The Fourth-Order Effects in the Michelson Interferometer

## Cite_Key
stavek2023descartes

## Problem
Predicting higher-order effects in Michelson interferometer assuming stationary luminiferous ether

## Method
Derives fourth-order (v/c)^4 effect in MMX; assumes stationary luminiferous ether; proposes LIGO test

## Data
Michelson interferometer configuration; LIGO parameters; predicted fringe shifts

## Metrics
Fourth-order effect magnitude; predicted fringe shift; testability with LIGO

## Findings
Fourth-order (v/c)^4 effect exists in MMX assuming stationary ether; LIGO could detect predicted fringe shift; alternative to SR explanation of MMX

## Limitations
Moderate quality (0.5); 3 citations; requires experimental validation
