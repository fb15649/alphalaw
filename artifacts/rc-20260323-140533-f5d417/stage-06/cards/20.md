# Is Birkhoff's Theorem Valid in Einstein-Aether Theory?

## Cite_Key
chan2022birkhoffs

## Problem
Determining validity of Birkhoff's theorem in Einstein-Aether modified gravity

## Method
Analysis of spherical vacuum solutions; conditions for staticity and flatness; study of horizons and singularities

## Data
Einstein-Aether field equations; vacuum solution parameters

## Metrics
Staticity conditions; flatness conditions; horizon structure

## Findings
Birkhoff's theorem modified in Einstein-Aether theory; conditions for staticity and flatness established; naked singularities and universal horizons possible
