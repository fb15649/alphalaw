# A New Explanation for the Result of Michelson-Morley Experiment

## Cite_Key
soltani2025explanation

## Problem
Explaining MMX null result without abandoning aether concept

## Method
Proposes 'Aether Attached to Earth' (AATE) theory; models luminiferous aether as transparent continuous medium attached to Earth's center

## Data
Michelson-Morley experimental configuration and results

## Metrics
Zero relative velocity prediction; consistency with null result

## Findings
If aether is attached to Earth's center, relative velocity between Earth and aether is zero; this naturally explains MMX null result without rejecting aether

## Limitations
Lower quality score (0.45); ad hoc theory; limited citations (1); no independent validation
