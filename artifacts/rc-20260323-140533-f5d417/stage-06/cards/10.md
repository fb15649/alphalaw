# Minimal Einstein-Aether Theory

## Cite_Key
gurses2024minimal

## Problem
Developing simplified aether theory within general relativity framework; reconciling cosmological constant problem

## Method
Modified gravity theory with aether vector field; shows reduction to Einstein equations with perfect fluid; mechanism for local Lorentz symmetry breaking

## Data
Cosmological observations; gravitational phenomena

## Metrics
Reduction to standard GR; cosmological constant mechanism; theoretical consistency

## Findings
Einstein-aether theory reduces to Einstein equations with perfect fluid contribution; provides mechanism for cosmological constant through local Lorentz symmetry breaking; maintains mathematical rigor

No citations yet; preprint stage; requires observational constraints
