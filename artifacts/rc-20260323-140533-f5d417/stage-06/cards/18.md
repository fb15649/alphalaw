# Constraining the parameters of generalized and viscous modified Chaplygin gas and black hole accretion in Einstein-Aether gravity

mukherjee2024constraining

## Problem
Understanding cosmic acceleration and black hole accretion within Einstein-Aether gravity framework

## Method
Einstein-Aether gravity applied to cosmological evolution; modified Chaplygin gas model; black hole accretion analysis

## Data
Cosmic acceleration observations; black hole accretion data; cosmological parameters

## Metrics
