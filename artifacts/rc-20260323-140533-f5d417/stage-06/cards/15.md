# Axionic extension of the Einstein-Dirac-aether theory: Degeneracy removal with respect to shear of the aether flow

## Cite_Key
efremova2023axionic

## Problem
Extending Einstein-Dirac-aether theory with axion field; understanding aether flow invariants

## Method
Einstein-Dirac-aether theory with dynamic aether and axion coupling; analysis of aether velocity invariants (expansion, shear, vorticity)

## Data
Theoretical framework; aether flow parameters

## Metrics
Degeneracy removal; axion coupling strength; theoretical consistency

## Findings
Axion field couples to aether shear; degeneracy in aether flow invariants removed by axion dynamics; expansion, shear, and vorticity characterize aether flow

No citations; highly specialized; preprint stage
