# Aether, Light; Unification of Physics Laws

## Cite_Key
niang2020aether

## Problem
Unifying gravitation and electromagnetic phenomena within single theoretical framework

## Method
Rehabilitates luminiferous ether concept; derives physical properties of ether; attempts proof using Einstein's equation

## Data
Gravitational and electromagnetic phenomena; Einstein's mass-energy equation

## Metrics
Derivation of physical properties; unification of laws

## Findings
Luminiferous ether can account for both gravitation and light properties; physical properties derivable from ether framework; Einstein's equation supports ether concept
