# Reinterpreting the Michelson-Morley Null Result: From "Material Aether" to "Vacuum Energy Density"

## Cite_Key
ilie2025reinterpreting

## Problem
The Michelson-Morley experiment null result led to rejection of luminiferous aether, but quantum vacuum energy presents new framework for understanding light propagation medium

## Method
Theoretical derivation treating vacuum as superfluid quantum medium; derives electromagnetic constants (ε₀, μ₀, Z₀) as elastic properties of vacuum medium

## Data
Michelson-Morley experimental results; quantum vacuum energy density data

## Metrics
Theoretical consistency with Maxwell's equations; derivation of electromagnetic constants from medium properties

## Findings
Vacuum can be understood as superfluid medium; ε₀, μ₀, Z₀ emerge as elastic properties of this medium; MMX null result compatible with quantum vacuum framework

## Limitations
Speculative theoretical framework; lacks direct experimental validation; date appears to be future (December 2025)
