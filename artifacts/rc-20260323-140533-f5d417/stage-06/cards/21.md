# Lorentz Symmetry Violation of Cosmic Photons

## Cite_Key
he2022lorentz

## Problem
Reviewing and testing Lorentz symmetry violation in cosmic photon propagation

## Method
Review of Lorentz symmetry violation theories; quantum gravity and effective field theory approaches; cosmic photon observations

## Data
Cosmic photon propagation data; quantum gravity models; Lorentz violation constraints

## Metrics
Lorentz violation bounds; theoretical model comparison; observational constraints

## Findings
Multiple frameworks predict Lorentz symmetry violation; cosmic photons provide sensitive tests; current bounds constrain but don't exclude aether-like theories

## Limitations
Review paper; 45 citations; doesn't directly support aether framework
