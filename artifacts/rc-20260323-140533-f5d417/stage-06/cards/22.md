# Exact Kerr-like solution and its shadow in a gravity model with spontaneous Lorentz symmetry breaking

## Cite_Key
ding2020exact

## Problem
Deriving rotating black hole solutions in Lorentz-violating gravity theories

## Method
Einstein-bumblebee gravity with spontaneous Lorentz symmetry breaking; Kerr-like solution derivation; black hole shadow analysis

## Data
Black hole shadow observations; Lorentz violation parameters

## Metrics
Shadow size modification; LV parameter constraints; consistency with observations

## Findings
Exact Kerr-like solution exists with Lorentz violation through vacuum expectation value; black hole shadow modified by LV parameter; similar effects to Einstein-aether black holes

## Limitations
Highly cited (126); bumblebee rather than aether framework; indirect relevance
